## Security and Privacy

### How do you protect my data?

Security measures include:
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Infrastructure**: Hosted on AWS with 99.99% uptime SLA
- **Backups**: Automatic daily backups with 30-day retention
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions, 2FA available
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA compliant

### Where is my data stored?

Data is stored in secure AWS data centers. You can choose your data region:
- United States (default)
- European Union (GDPR compliance)
- Canada
- Australia
- United Kingdom

Enterprise customers can request specific regions or on-premise deployment.

### Do you sell my data?

Absolutely not. We never sell user data to third parties. We don't show ads. Our business model is subscription-based - we succeed when you succeed.

### Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your data at any time in standard formats:
- Projects and tasks: CSV, JSON
- Documents: Original file formats
- Messages: JSON, HTML
- Analytics: CSV, Excel

Go to Settings > Data Export to download your data.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

After cancellation:
- You have 30 days to export your data
- After 30 days, data is archived for 90 days
- After 120 days total, data is permanently deleted
- You can request early deletion anytime

## Account Management

### How do I add or remove team members?

Admins can manage team members in Settings > Team:
1. Click "Invite Member"
2. Enter email address and select role
3. Member receives invitation email
4. They create account and join your workspace

To remove: Click on member > Remove from team

### What user roles are available?

- **Admin**: Full access, can manage billing and team members
- **Manager**: Can create projects, manage assigned teams
- **Member**: Can work on assigned projects and tasks
- **Guest**: Limited access to specific projects only (Pro+ plans)

### Can I have multiple workspaces?

Yes! Each workspace is a separate account with its own team, projects, and billing. You can switch between workspaces from the top menu. Useful for:
- Separating clients (agencies)
- Different departments (enterprises)
- Personal vs. professional use

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:
1. Export your data (if needed)
2. Go to Settings > Account
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone after 30 days.

## Troubleshooting

### Why can't I log in?

Common solutions:
1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Try password reset
3. Check if your organization has SSO enabled
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser or incognito mode

### Why is the app slow?

Troubleshooting steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Check our status page: https://status.example.com

If problems persist, contact support with details about your browser and what actions are slow.

### Why didn't I receive my invitation email?

Check:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email address spelling
3. Corporate email filters

Still missing? Contact support and we'll resend or provide alternative signup method.
